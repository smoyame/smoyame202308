Pardee Annual Report

SUMMARY

I formatted Denver University's Pardee Center for International Futures' Annual Report, working strictly within their brand guidelines.

See the Report Pardee Center for International Futures' 2020‒2021 Annual Report ↗

I created a couple concepts and then moved forward with the concept chosen by the client, making sure to implement critique from our Art Director along the way. Even while waiting for photography and statistics to be finalized for publication, we made sure to design for their eventual presentation from the start.
