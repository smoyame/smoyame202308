Wavelength

SUMMARY

Keeping up the fight for reproductive rights and constitutional freedoms during the 2024 General Election.

For the record Google Ads Entry ↗

I joined Wavelength's nimble team as a Senior Designer during the 2024 presidential general election cycle. My responsibilities included art direction, illustration, storyboard and design for down-ballot issues and voter mobilization across Ohio, Arizona, Nevada, Missouri, and Maryland.

I also leveraged my software skills to develop, code, and design an MVP for a Photoshop plugin to automate the display ad resizing process.
