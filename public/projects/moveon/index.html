MoveOn

SUMMARY

MoveOn is a progressive political action non-profit that was established in 1998, championing digital diligency that can affect political change.

See the Movement MoveOn.org ↗

While there are some items I cannot show, I have a few social media contributions that are floating out there!

Reproductive rights, primaries, student debt relief, and more. I helped MoveOn and its member's voices show up by designing social media assets and merchandise during crucial current events in the summer of 2022.

As an on-going client of the agency I worked for, there was no shortage of action or voices that we wanted to empower.
