Amass Magazine

SUMMARY

I was challenged to create a visual hierarchy for a publication during the initial COVID lockdown.

AMASS magazine is a 14-page publication concept made to capture the voices of those less heard and immortalize the infamous that wring them dry. Its content focuses on the divide between actions and consequences when they affect the average American instead of a fellow corporate partner.
